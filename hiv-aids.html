Kikundi cha Maisha Bora

The HIV/AIDS support group, "Kikundi cha Maisha Bora" (Group for Better Life) was established in 2009 as a Peace Corps project. The group has grown drastically to nearly 80 members; an all-inclusive group of people living with and without HIV/AIDS. Currently, the group has a choir and drama troupe, teaching villagers and students about HIV transmission, importance of testing, and availability of HIV medications. As part of a Wangama Foundation initiative, the Group for Better Life has a bee keeping business.

Beekeeping Business:

20 beekeeping boxes provided to the group to start the business

Begin selling honey in 2019 for income-generation

Group for Better Life also uses honey for immune boosting qualities

Future Plans:

As the beekeeping project grows, we wish to continue supporting the group with personal protective equipment

Obtain honey straining equipment

Income generation and business management education

Continuing education of people living with HIV/AIDS (nutrition, dental care, opportunistic infections, sexual health)
