About

In Wangama Ward, 147 total orphans attend the three schools, posing a challenge for uniform and school fees for these students and the schools. Three students have a disability and are in need of extra support at the schools. All students share textbooks in the classroom; currently 1 textbook for 7 students.

In order to create opportunities for education for the students, our educational projects include: textbooks, assistance with school and uniform fees for orphans and vulnerable children, and school supplies. With access to the right resources, the students of Wangama can become empowered by their own abilities and gain the confidence to fulfill their potential.

Wangama Foundation Provided:

  • Funds for 24 textbooks donated to the 3 primary schools (2018) 126 notebooks given to orphans at the 3 primary schools (2018)
  • Funds provided to each of the 3 primary schools in Wangama Ward for support of children with special needs
  • Laptop donated to Wangama Primary School (2019)
  • Donated backpacks, pencils, sharpeners, chalk, erasers, notebooks for all orphans at 3 primary schools (2019)
  • 33 trees to each of the 3 schools for orphans avocado tree project, totaling 99 trees (2019)

Future Plans:

  • Technical or secondary school sponsorship of orphans
  • Avocado tree project expansion and business development education
